Whale Hunter is a tool that calculates the price of a cryptocurrency and compares it against an expected percentage, this in order to be able to have an alert when an asset is moving quickly. The following development is an experiment and it is not advised to do operations with it, use it at your own risk and draw your own conclusions. Currently it only operates by reviewing the price of coins in Binance futures, in case you want other assets or exchanges, contact: CryptoValueFighter


  • Interval/seconds: The duration time between each price query
  • Expected percentage change: Variation of the percentage calculated with the last reported price
